Frequently Asked Questions

Is Twick free?

Yes! The core functionality is free. We offer a Pro tier for power users who want chat history, timestamps, and custom themes.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Twick stores all settings locally on your device via iCloud. We do not track your messages or collect personal data.

Do I need accounts?

No login is required to read chats. You can simply add channels and start watching the conversation immediately.

Why use Twick?

The official apps don't support third-party emotes (7TV) or combined chats. Twick fills that gap for the mobile community.
